## üßµ Approved Filament Brands

### Premium Brands (Preferred)
- **Prusament** (Prusa Research)
- **Polymaker** (PolyTerra, PolyLite, PolyMax)
- **BambuLab** (Bambu PLA, PETG, etc.)
- **Hatchbox**
- **eSUN** (ePLA, ePETG, etc.)
- **Overture**
- **Sunlu**
- **Polymaker**

### Quality Brands (Accepted)
- **ColorFabb**
- **Fillamentum**
- **FormFutura**
- **Taulman3D** (for specialty materials)
- **MatterHackers**
- **Atomic Filament**

### Avoid (Noname/Low Quality)
- Generic/unknown brands
- Extremely cheap filaments (< 10 GEL/kg typically)
- No-name Chinese brands without reputation
- Unbranded filaments

### Notes
- "Other" option available with brand name and justification
- Admin reviews "other" brands on case-by-case basis
- Quality matters more than brand - if maker can prove quality, consider it
- Material type matters: PLA, PETG, ABS, TPU generally accepted
